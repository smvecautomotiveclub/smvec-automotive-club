■ Who we are

More than a club —
a racing team

SMVEC Automotive Club brings together students passionate about motorsport and engineering. Members form competition teams for events like Go-Kart and Quad races, building everything from scratch.

⚙
Build from Scratch
Every competition vehicle is designed, fabricated and tuned by our students.
🏁
National Competitions
We compete in Go-Kart, BAJA SAE, Quad and more across India.
👥
Team Formation
Club members form teams of ~25 with specialized sub-roles for each competition.
🏆
Career Edge
Real project experience and industry exposure that sets our graduates apart.
